A great alternative to your regular pizza

By: LyraThemes

Ingredinets

For Crust

1 (12inch) pre-baked pizza crust

1/2 cup pesto

Toppingst

1 ripe tomato,chopped

1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped

1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained

1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced

1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions

  • Step1Preheat oven to 459 degress F (230degress C)
  • Step2Spread pesto on pizza crust
  • Step3 Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese.
  • Step4Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or cheese is melted and browned
